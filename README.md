# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance, profitability, order trends, and product category insights. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Monitor overall business performance using key metrics.
* Analyze monthly sales and profit trends.
* Identify top-performing categories and sub-categories.
* Track Year-over-Year (YoY) growth.
* Evaluate regional and state-wise sales performance.
* Enable interactive filtering for better decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. KPI Summary Cards

Provides a quick overview of overall business performance.

### 2. Sales & Profit Analysis

Monthly comparison of sales and profit to identify trends and seasonality.

### 3. Category-wise Profit Analysis

Shows profit contribution across product categories.

### 4. Category-wise Sales Share

Visual representation of category sales distribution.

### 5. State-wise Sales Analysis

Geographical analysis of sales performance across states.

### 6. Top 10 Sub-Categories

Highlights the highest revenue-generating product sub-categories.

### 7. Interactive Filters

Users can filter data by:

* Year
* Region
* Customer Segment

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Total Sales: ₹22.97 Lakhs
* Total Profit: ₹2.86 Lakhs
* Profit Margin: 12.47%
* Sales Growth: 20.62% YoY
* Phones and Chairs emerged as top-selling sub-categories.
* Technology category contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Regional analysis revealed high-performing states and growth opportunities.
